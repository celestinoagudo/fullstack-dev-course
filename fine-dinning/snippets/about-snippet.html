About Us

The company started wayback 1945, after Hermohenes De Ezpadaña a well known chef in Spain befriended the Filipino - American occupation troops stationed at Sampaloc Manila. Because of this friendship, the group regularly visited Hermohenes’ nearby home for food and drinks they even requests Hermohenes for what food to eat.

Later on, the troops insisted that they pay for their foods and drinks. This prompted Hermohenes to open a canteen, where the group could enjoy foods and drinks.

Later, an idea has arrived for Hermohenes to present foods for breakfast, lunch and supper where the group delightedly agreed. Over the years,Exquisite Cuisine Company’s popularity grew and it became known as “Sta Mesa’s most acclaimed food subscription company.”

Exquisite Cuisine has established itself as a household name in Manila, an institution, and a proud Spain tradition. The second and third generations of the family continue to zealously uphold the standards and traditions set by Hermohenes for all Exquisite Cuisine Branches.
